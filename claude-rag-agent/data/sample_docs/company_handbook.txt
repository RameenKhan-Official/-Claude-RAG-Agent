Acme Robotics Employee Handbook (Sample)

Section 1: Time Off Policy
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, credited
monthly. PTO requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance
through the HR portal. Unused PTO up to 5 days may be carried over into the
next calendar year; any remaining balance is forfeited.

Section 2: Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP-level sign-off and are reviewed
quarterly. All remote employees must be reachable during core hours,
10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local time zone.

Section 3: Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses under $75 can be self-approved and submitted via the
expense portal with a receipt. Expenses of $75 or more require manager
approval before submission. Reimbursements are processed within 10
business days of approval.

Section 4: Equipment
New hires receive a laptop, monitor, and a one-time $300 home-office
stipend within their first 30 days. Equipment refresh cycles occur every
3 years for laptops and every 5 years for monitors.

Section 5: Performance Reviews
Formal performance reviews occur twice a year, in June and December.
Managers and employees each complete a self-assessment and peer feedback
form two weeks prior to the review meeting.
